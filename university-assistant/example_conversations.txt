--- Conversation 4: Attendance Warning ---

Student: My attendance in CS401 is 68%. Can I still give the end-semester exam?

UniAssist: I can see this is a concern, Amit. Here's the situation:

The university policy requires a minimum of 75% attendance to appear for end-semester exams. At 68%, you are currently below the required threshold.

YOUR OPTIONS:
1. Condonation Request — You can apply to your HOD (Dr. Anita Sharma for CSE) if:
   • You have genuine reasons (medical, official duty)
   • Shortage is within the condonable limit (usually up to 10%, so 65–74%)
   • At 68% you are within the condonable range

2. What to do NOW:
   • Write a formal application to the HOD with supporting documents
   • If medical: attach doctor's certificate (within 3 working days of absence)
   • Submit before the exam hall ticket release date

3. If not condoned:
   • You will be debarred from the exam
   • Supplementary exam option available after results

Please act quickly — contact Dr. Anita Sharma (anita.sharma@university.edu) today.
