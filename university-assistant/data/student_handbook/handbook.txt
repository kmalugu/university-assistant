UNIVERSITY STUDENT HANDBOOK

----------------------------------------
1. INTRODUCTION
----------------------------------------
Welcome to the University Student Handbook. This guide provides essential information about academic policies, campus facilities, student responsibilities, and support services. Students are expected to read and follow all guidelines mentioned in this handbook.

----------------------------------------
2. ACADEMIC STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------
The academic year is divided into two semesters:
- Odd Semester (July – November)
- Even Semester (January – May)

Each course is assigned credits based on workload. Students must complete the required credits to graduate.

Grading System:
A+ : 9.0 – 10.0
A  : 8.0 – 8.9
B+ : 7.0 – 7.9
B  : 6.0 – 6.9
C  : 5.0 – 5.9
F  : Fail

----------------------------------------
3. ATTENDANCE POLICY
----------------------------------------
- Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory for all courses.
- Students below 75% may not be allowed to sit for examinations.
- Medical leave must be supported with valid documents.
- Attendance is monitored regularly.

----------------------------------------
4. EXAMINATION RULES
----------------------------------------
- Students must carry their ID cards to the examination hall.
- Late entry beyond 30 minutes is not allowed.
- Malpractice (cheating) will lead to strict disciplinary action.
- Electronic devices are strictly prohibited.

----------------------------------------
5. CODE OF CONDUCT
----------------------------------------
Students are expected to:
- Maintain discipline on campus.
- Respect faculty, staff, and fellow students.
- Avoid any form of harassment or misconduct.
- Follow ethical academic practices.

Strictly Prohibited:
- Ragging (will result in immediate suspension or expulsion)
- Substance abuse (alcohol, drugs, smoking)
- Violence or vandalism

----------------------------------------
6. CAMPUS FACILITIES
----------------------------------------
Library:
- Open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM
- Students can borrow up to 5 books

Laboratories:
- Access allowed only during scheduled hours
- Proper lab safety protocols must be followed

Hostel:
- Entry time: 9:00 PM
- Visitors allowed between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM
- Students must adhere to hostel rules

----------------------------------------
7. PLACEMENT AND CAREER SERVICES
----------------------------------------
The placement cell assists students with:
- Internship opportunities
- Resume building
- Mock interviews
- Campus recruitment drives

Eligibility:
- Minimum CGPA of 6.5 (may vary by company)
- No active backlogs

----------------------------------------
8. SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID
----------------------------------------
Types of scholarships:
- Merit-based scholarships
- Need-based financial assistance
- Government-funded scholarships

Students must apply through the university portal before deadlines.

----------------------------------------
9. STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES
----------------------------------------
Counseling Services:
- Available for mental health and academic stress

Grievance Cell:
- Students can report issues related to academics or campus life

Anti-Ragging Committee:
- Strictly monitors and prevents ragging activities

----------------------------------------
10. DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS
----------------------------------------
Violation of rules may result in:
- Warning
- Suspension
- Expulsion (in severe cases)
