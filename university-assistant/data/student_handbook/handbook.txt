WELCOME TO UNIVERSITY STUDENT HANDBOOK 2024-25
================================================

ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY
---------------------
Our university is a premier institution of higher learning offering programs in Technology, Management, and Sciences. Established in 1985, we have been ranked among the top 50 universities in India by NIRF.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS OFFERED
--------------------------
Undergraduate:
- B.Tech in Computer Science (4 years)
- B.Tech in Electronics & Communication (4 years)
- B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (4 years)
- BSc in Data Science (3 years)

Postgraduate:
- M.Tech in Computer Science (2 years)
- MSc in Data Science (2 years)
- MBA (2 years - Evening/Weekend for working professionals)
- MSc in Applied Mathematics (2 years)

Doctoral:
- PhD in all major departments (3-6 years)

Exchange Programs:
- Semester exchange with 15+ international partner universities
- Students can spend 1-2 semesters abroad while maintaining enrollment

STUDENT SERVICES
-----------------
Academic Advising:
Each student is assigned a Faculty Advisor who helps with course selection, academic planning, and career guidance. Students should meet their advisor at least once per semester.

Career Services (Placement Cell):
- Resume review and mock interviews
- Company registration and on-campus recruitment
- Alumni networking events
- Industry internship assistance
Location: Admin Block, Ground Floor | Email: placement@university.edu

Financial Aid Office:
- Scholarship applications and disbursements
- Student loan assistance and documentation
- Financial hardship support
Location: Admin Block, Room 102 | Hours: 9 AM to 5 PM (Mon-Fri)

International Student Cell (ISC):
- Visa and immigration support
- Cultural adjustment programs
- Buddy program (pairing new international students with local students)
Location: Admin Block, Room 101 | Email: isc@university.edu
